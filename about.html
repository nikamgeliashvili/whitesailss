about us

WHITE SAILS is a perfect opportunity for those who prefer to live away from city dust and noise. Sea, park and ecologically clean environment, stunning views and modern infrastructure create perfect living conditions and atmosphere.

The complex provides all essential facilities necessary for holidays or everyday life: outdoor and indoor swimming pools, tennis courts, bowling center, gyms, spa centers, restaurants, shops, children playground, green space, casinos and others. Our fully maintained apartments will be equipped with Smart House system, which enables you to control your apartment from any location.

According to the study of financial corporation Doing Business, Georgia ranks 8th in Ease of Doing Business. It greatly contributes to investment opportunities and rapid economic growth of the country. The following factors are of huge importance:

  • No Property Taxes
  • Fast and Safe property registration system
  • Electronic taxation system and no corruption
  • Legalized gambling (unlike other countries in the region)
